## Fisherman upon the waters

【宋代】范仲淹\
Song dynasty: Fan Zhongyan

江上往來人，但愛鱸魚美。\
Those passerby by the riverside, savor their fresh-caught perch.

君看一葉舟，出沒風波里。\
That boat which appears as a leaf, braves the waves to receive these.
